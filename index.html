About

This is a small studio of paper and watercolor. Every piece begins with a feeling before it becomes a form. Nothing here is loud. Everything here is meant to be held slowly.

Notes from the Studio

The brush does not ask permission before it moves.
It leans into water the way memory leans into silence.
And somehow, something beautiful appears anyway.

Some colors arrive like visitors.
They stay long enough to be remembered
and then they leave without saying goodbye.

I tried to make everything perfect.
But the paint preferred honesty.
